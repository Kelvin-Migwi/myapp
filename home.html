JKUMSA (JKUAT Muslim Students Association)

JKUMSA is a Students Association that brings together Muslims in JKUAT and in Juja.
It includes all JKUAT students, teaching and non-teaching staff and Juja locals.

THE GOAL OF JKUMSA

Motto

Hold fast to the covenant of Allah and do not be disunited

Vision

To represent and support an inclusive vibrant students community within the University

Mission

To unify Muslim students within University Environment towards the cause of Islamic brotherhood as well as to nurture and develop potential Muslim leaders in present and future

Core Values

Unity, brotherhood, respect, integrity
